About the Book

I wrote this book as an act of obedience. During a season where I lost my income of 17 years, when all I wanted to do was figure out how to rebuild, God said -- stay on the wall and write this book. So I did.

GOD, I WANT TO HEAR YOU gives every believer nine essential practices for positioning themselves to seek, hear, and obey God -- no matter what season they are in.

"Hearing God is not reserved for the spiritually elite. It is the birthright of every believer."

My Story
Built on obedience,
not just strategy.

I am a Christian author, speaker, entrepreneur, and strategist who teaches believers how to hear God's voice and apply His direction to every area of their lives.

"My $12 million network marketing career was not built on strategy. It was built on learning to hear and obey God's voice."

My journey from $232,000 in IRS debt and a home in foreclosure to building an organization of over 100,000 distributors is not a business story. It is a faith story. And for more than thirteen years I have hosted The Daily Prayer Call, a free weekday gathering where believers come together to seek God and learn to hear His voice.

I live in Franklin, Tennessee and I love spending time with my children and grandchildren, traveling, and documenting my own God stories.
